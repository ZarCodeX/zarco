Welcome to Zarco Discord App

Zarco is a multipurpose Discord app designed to enhance your server's functionality. For optimal performance, we recommend granting admin permissions during the bot invite process. Additionally, ensure that the bot's role is moved to the top of your server's role hierarchy so that it can operate seamlessly without any issues.

Invite Zarco

How to Get Started

  1. Click the invite button above to add Zarco to your Discord server.
  2. Grant the necessary permissions (admin is suggested) to ensure full functionality.
  3. After inviting the bot, move its role to the top of the role hierarchy to avoid any issues.
  4. Follow the in-app instructions to configure the bot and explore its multipurpose features.
